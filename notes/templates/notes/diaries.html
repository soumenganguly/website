Diaries

  • The Power of Habit: I have been recently trying to understand the causes of laziness and the ways to overcome it. A steady state of life leads to a more productive way of living your life. And, to attain a steady state you'll need to practice the 'Scheduling Algorithm of Life'. But, ofcourse randomness is also the source of finding new ways to an exciting and productive life, once in a while you'll need to break free and look for whats beyond your horizon. Let me talk more about finding a steady state in life.

    This is how I am trying to maintain a steady state:

  • Wake up early in the morning.(Sync your clock with the Sun and productivity will flow automatically).
  • Maintain a calendar for your day-to-day activities.[Preference- Thunderbird]
  • Have a clear picture of your objectives and sort them by priority.
  • Contribute/Donate to any organisation that is serving towards your passion. For me: OpenStreetMap(Connecting the world), SOS Childrens Village(Education for all).
  • Read. Read. Read. A book/novel of your liking EVERYDAY.
  • Last but not the least, spend time with your loved ones.
